Our Mission

Food as a Force for Good

Damal was founded not just as a restaurant, but as a community hub. We are committed to supporting our local East London community through a range of charitable initiatives — from feeding the vulnerable to supporting Somali youth organisations.

With every order you place, a portion goes towards our community fund. Together, we can make a real difference.

Our Initiatives

🍱
Feed the Hungry
Every Friday, we prepare and distribute free meals to individuals experiencing homelessness in Whitechapel and Tower Hamlets. No one should go hungry.
Every Friday · Whitechapel
🎓
Youth Mentorship
We partner with local Somali youth organisations to provide mentorship, work experience, and hospitality training for young people aged 16–24 in East London.
Monthly programme · Ages 16–24
🌍
Somalia Relief
1% of every dine-in meal goes to our Somalia relief fund, supporting communities in Mogadishu and Hargeisa through partner NGOs working on food security and education.
